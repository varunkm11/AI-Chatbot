How to Embed

Add the chatbot to your website in just 3 simple steps:

Step 1: Include the Script

<script src="https://your-domain.com/static/chatbot-widget.js"></script>

Step 2: Add the Data Attribute (Auto-Initialize)

<div data-chatbot data-api-url="http://localhost:8000" data-position="bottom-right" data-theme="light" data-model="mistralai/mistral-7b-instruct" data-title="AI Assistant" data-placeholder="Type your message..."></div>

Step 3: Or Initialize Manually

const chatbot = new ChatbotWidget({ apiUrl: 'http://localhost:8000', position: 'bottom-right', theme: 'light', model: 'mistralai/mistral-7b-instruct', title: 'AI Assistant', placeholder: 'Type your message...' });
